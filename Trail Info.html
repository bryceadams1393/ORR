Trail Information

Explore our trail system connecting Northern Illinois and Southern Wisconsin. Trails are open from December 15 through March 15 with at least 4" of snow. Please obey all signage and respect landowner property.

For new snowmobilers, we get permission from private land owners to use their land for our enjoyment. Snowmobilers should stay within the markers and not wonder off the marked trails. Some of the land has winter wheat under the snow and riding over these fields cause damage to their crops. Clubs have to pay for the damages and we can even loose our privilage to ride on their land.

Roscoe, IL Ordinance:

  • Sunday - Thursday 8:00am - 10:00pm
  • Friday and Saturday 8:00am - 12:00pm

You have to be off the trail by these times or if caught, you will be ticketed.
